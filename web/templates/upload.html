How to Upload a List of DOIs

  • Open a blank workbook in Microsoft Excel.
  • In the first column, list the DOIs you want to search. The following formats can be used:
    • 10.1001/jama.280.3.229
    • doi:10.1001/jama.280.3.231
    • https://doi.org/10.1001/archinte.159.10.1082
  • Save and Upload.
